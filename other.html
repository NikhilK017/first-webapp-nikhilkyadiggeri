Other Resources

Khan Academy is a completely free learning platform that breaks down complex topics into simple, engaging lessons. Whether you're catching up on algebra, exploring science, or preparing for a big test, it offers video tutorials and practice that feel like having a personal tutor.

Britannica is like having an entire encyclopedia at your fingertips—only faster and smarter. Written and reviewed by experts, it's a trusted go-to when you want solid, factual information without the noise of random internet opinions.

Kahoot! brings energy to learning. It's an interactive quiz platform where you can play or create games on anything from math facts to movie trivia. Great for classrooms, study groups, or anyone who thinks learning should be fun and fast-paced.

Sporcle is the ultimate site for trivia fans. Whether you’re testing your geography chops or trying to name every movie Tom Hanks has starred in, Sporcle’s got a quiz for it. Warning: it’s highly addictive—in the best way!
